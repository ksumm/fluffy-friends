ABOUT US:


Founded in 2022, FLUFFY FRIENDS LIMERICK takes care to dogs in needs. Our mission: to protect, provide, shelter, and care to the dogs, find them HOME and promote human care for dogs across the Ireland.

PROGRAMS WE SUPPORT:


  • Animal Food Programs
  • Therapy and Care Dogs Programs
  • Keeping Dogs Whith Their Owners Programs
