ABOUT US:

Founded in 2022, FLUFFY FRIENDS LIMERICK takes care to dogs in needs. Our mission: to protect, provide, shelter, and support to the dogs, find them HOME and promote human care for our fluffy friends across the Ireland.

PROGRAMS WE SUPPORT:

  • Rehoming Dogs Programs
  • Therapy and Care Dogs Programs
  • Keeping Dogs Whith Their Owners Programs
  • Animal Food Programs

These animals could really use some love.

Our goal is to find loving, stable homes for the pets in our care. Ready to adopt or foster your new best friend? Start looking!
